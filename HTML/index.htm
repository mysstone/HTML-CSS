What does this website do?

The StudentClub is UC Denver's place for student fun. College is more than classroom lectures, library research, term papers and final exams that lead to a degree. College is about enhancing your experiences through involvement and engagement in campus activities, taking new adventures and forming friendships to last a lifetime. College is about accepting personal challenges, broadening your skills and making your future your reality!

Join the StudentClub to enhance your campus experiences, meet new people and just have a good time.
