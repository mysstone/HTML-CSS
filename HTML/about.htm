About us

Welcome to the CU Denver Club page! On this site you will find all your needs regarding club meet up times, membership details, and other endevours. Since 2018, CU Denver Club has expanded greatly, and we now sponsor over 50 unique clubs! Founded by two CU Denver students who wanted to connect the CU Denver System's clubs together, we now provide service to the whole student body of CU Denver.
